Penanggungan Summit Explore with AI Customer Service

Penanggunga Summit Explore is a landing page that provides information about climbing Mount Penanggungan in Mojokerto, East Java, Indonesia, and is equipped with an AI Chatbot Customer Service.

Year

2025

Category

College Assignment

Services

Web with AI Chatbot

Description

Software engineering assignment to create an integrated platform with artificial intelligence. This project was carried out as a team, and I was responsible for the artificial intelligence component.

The Summit Explore platform is a static website that does not use a database. The front-end technologies used are HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript. This platform also features an AI Chatbot Customer Service that uses the chatbase.co platform for its AI Chatbot Customer Service creation.
