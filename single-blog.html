4th place in the Wheeled Soccer Robot Competition at the 2024 Indonesian Robot Contest

A valuable experience for my career, participating and contributing to the Indonesian Robot Competition in the Wheeled Soccer Robot Division (KRSBI - B) organized by PUSPRESNAS in 2024.

4th place in the Wheeled Soccer Robot Competition at the 2024 Indonesian Robot Contest

Experience and Achievements

My initial national-level robotics experience, which left a profound impression, was participating in "Kontes Robot Indonesia" (KRI), an annual competition hosted by the National Achievement Center "PUSPRESNAS." As a member of the Dewo Robotic Unesa Bratasena KRSBI Beroda Team, a team still in its learning phase, my primary responsibility was to research and continuously enhance the computer vision programs for our robots, aiming for ever-improving features.

This dedication to refining our robotic vision paid off significantly. In the competition, our Bratasena KRSBI Beroda Team achieved a commendable 4th place at the Regional Level II.

Furthermore, we successfully replicated this strong performance, securing another 4th place at the National Level. This experience not only solidified my passion for robotics but also provided invaluable insights into competitive development and teamwork.
